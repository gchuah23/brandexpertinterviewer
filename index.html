About Gerald Chuah & My Approach

Based in Kuala Lumpur, Gerald Chuah is the go-to pro for turning complex ideas into compelling narratives. With 25+ years in print, digital and broadcast journalism—and as founder of Brandizing Solutions—he helps leaders, innovators and brands tell stories that stick.

My Philosophy: Listening First

As a longtime journalist, I know that not everyone is articulate or knows how to express themselves—especially when it comes to talking about their brand, products, or services. It can be quite a hassle, and you can spend long hours toiling at it without getting to the point. It’s not your fault, because not everyone is born a writer, and when it comes to putting your thoughts into writing, there’s always a fear of making mistakes. That’s what stops most people from sharing their wisdom and knowledge, so they end up doing the bare minimum without telling the world who they really are or what they’re capable of achieving.

That’s where the role of a writer comes into play. A writer is not just a machine or a typewriter; a writer also listens, finds your voice, and understands your journey, struggles, beliefs, and unique selling points—those gems that make you different from your competitors. That’s where your true value lies. It can be hard to praise yourself in that way—you need someone to give you a listening ear and truly understand your story before telling it to the world. My job is to help you be natural, be yourself, and be powerful—not by exaggerating, but by ensuring your story is never boring.
